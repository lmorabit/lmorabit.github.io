Thesis Dress

When a PhD candidate defends her thesis at Leiden, she is expected to wear something comparable to 'white tie' for a man. I wanted something special for such a special day, and decided to make my own dress. I used a vintage Vogue pattern (V2903), with black raw silk. Before sewing the dress together, I embroidered details on it based on my thesis work. I used a metallic silver embroidery thread from DMC. After transferring the patterns to the dress pieces, all the embroidery was done by hand. Once completed, I pieced the dress together. The results were all I could have hoped for -- and very memorable!
